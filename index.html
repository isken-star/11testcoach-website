How it works

Three steps to calmer 11+ prep.

STEP 01

Set up in minutes

Add your child, pick your target schools from the built-in database of 248, and choose a Parent Mode PIN. The app configures the right subjects automatically.

STEP 02

A little every day

Your child follows their daily plan with Taco — bite-size practice, streaks and XP. Baselines and mock tests slot in when you're ready.

STEP 03

You stay in the picture

Parent Mode shows exactly where they're strong and what to practise next — with reminders, assignments, rewards and shareable PDF progress reports.

Common questions

Quick answers.

Which schools and regions are covered?

248 selective schools: grammar schools across England, partially selective schools, and Northern Ireland's SEAG schools. Each entry knows which subjects that school tests, so practice stays focused.

Which devices does it run on?

iPhone and iPad. The app is designed for both, including full support for iPad layouts in portrait and landscape.

Does my child need an account or an internet connection?

No accounts at all, and once installed the app works fully offline. All data stays on the device — see our privacy policy.

Is it suitable for younger children to use on their own?

Yes — the child experience is friendly, self-contained and free of external links. Settings, data and anything parent-facing sit behind a PIN-protected Parent Mode.
